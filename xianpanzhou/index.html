About Me

Hello! I am currently a researcher in the Tencent Hunyuan team. My primary areas of interest in research are multimodal generation and its applications. I obtained M.S. degrees from Zhejiang University, advised by Professor Xi Li. Prior to that, I obtained my bachelor degree from Sichuan University.

Vision plays a significant role in human senses. My work is dedicated to providing users with better product experiences in various application scenarios based on the latest cutting-edge research.
